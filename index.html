Why Choose Greenfield?

  • CBSE-aligned curriculum with strong STEM focus.
  • Low student–teacher ratio for personal attention.
  • Clubs for robotics, arts, music, and sports.

Admission Process

  1. Submit the online enquiry form.
  2. Visit campus and interact with the admission team.
  3. Appear for a short interaction/assessment.
  4. Complete documentation and fee payment.

Help / FAQ

GPS-enabled transport ensures safe pickup and drop across the city.

CCTV cameras and regular safety drills keep the campus secure.

What are the school timings?

Regular timings are 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM, Monday to Friday. Activity clubs run till 4:00 PM on selected days.

Is transportation available?

Yes, GPS-enabled buses cover major routes across the city with trained staff on board.

What safety measures are followed?

CCTV surveillance, verified staff, infirmary, and regular safety drills are part of our safety framework.
